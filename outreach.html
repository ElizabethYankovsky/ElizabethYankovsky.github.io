Outreach

Performing an ocean acidification experiment at NJ Ocean Fun Days 2019. Universal pH indicator is added to three cups containing tap water. Lemon juice is added to one (turning the solution red) and a volunteer is asked to blow bubbles (introducing carbon dioxide) into a second cup. As this is done, the water turns yellow, indicating acidification - analogous to the process of carbon uptake by the ocean.
